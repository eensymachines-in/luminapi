 Autolumin

Scheduled automatic illumination for your homes

Autolumin is a tiny control device that can follow a set schedule of your home's illumination. Set the schedule and forget it. You are relieved of your duties of keeping a track of time. Autolumin can have 4 groups of lights that can be controlled independently and parallel. Autolumin uses internet to let you command the device from just about anywhere (there is internet). Want to change your schedule when away from home ? - pick up your mobile, change the schedule and your device can follow.
